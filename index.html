Features

Highly Customizable

Create a simple workout with just a few taps of create a custom workout that gives you complete control of every interval.

Standalone Apple Watch Workouts

Leave your phone at home! Great for runners.

Spoken Interval  Prompts

Intervals speaks your interval names so you can leave your phone in your pocket. There’s even an option to tell you the upcoming interval.

Apple Health Integration

Save workouts, heart rate, calories, distance, pace, and route maps to Apple Health.

Alert Sounds

Pick from a variety of alert sounds for times when you don’t want spoken prompts.  Great for meditation and  yoga workouts.

Workout History

View past workouts in Intervals or the Apple Activity App.  All workout data is stored securely in Apple Health.

Alert Sounds

Pick from a variety of alert sounds for times when you don’t want spoken prompts.  Great for meditation and  yoga workouts.

Remote Apple Watch View

Start a workout on you watch and view it live on your iPhone. Get the best of Intervals on Apple Watch and iPhone at the same time.

Runs with the Screen Locked

Intervals runs in the background so you can leave your phone in your pocket.

Hands Free Workout with Siri

Use Siri on iPhone or Apple Watch for a hands free workout. “Start workout with Intervals”.

Play Music or Podcasts

Use the app of your choice to play music or podcasts during your workout.  By default, Intervals will temporarily lower the volume so you can hear Interval prompts.

iCloud Support

Timers can be automatically saved to iCloud to sync between devices or be restored on a new phone.
